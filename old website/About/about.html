About Me

My name is Brandon, as you have probably identified by now.
And I am a first-year student at The University of Applied Sciences
studying User Experience Design. I am currently searching
for an internship position. I focus on UX research,
understanding the user, the thought process behind the ideation phases.
I want to delve deeper into the development scene,
so I am very interested in learning more coding languages.
I was born and raised in a beautiful,
diverse country called Namibia, and this
has shaped me into a culturally aware person. I always
shared a passion for helping people and trying
to explain things in a manner that everyone can understand.
I find it extremely exciting trying to understand
how people interact with different products
