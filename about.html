About Me

My name is Brandon Grimm as you have probably identified by now. I was born and raised in a beautiful diverse country called Namibia and I am studying at The Hague University of Applied Sciences undertaking the major User Experience Design. I am currently looking to expand my knowledge as a Junior UX Designer within an Internship role.

I have had a deep fascination towards the interaction between a person and a piece of technology. Understanding the user, is crucial part of UX and this is what led my passion for UX as from a young age I was fascinated watching my dad playing video games seeing the different techniques used to create a more immersive world.

This shaped my desire to be involved with something that creates an meaningful experience, an experience shaped by the user and his needs. I always shared a this passion to help others, to explain in a way that that individual would understand. I find it extremely gratifying to see people grasp the concept of a product without much explanation.
