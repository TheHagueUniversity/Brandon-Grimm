About Me

My name is Brandon Grimm as you have probably identified by now. I was born and raised in a beautifully diverse country called Namibia. Currently, I am studying at The Hague University of Applied Sciences undertaking the major User Experience Design. I am currently looking to expand my knowledge as a Junior UX Designer within an Internship role.


I have had a deep fascination towards the interaction between a person and a piece of technology. Understanding the user is a crucial part of UX and this is what led my passion for UX as from a young age I was fascinated by watching my dad playing video games seeing the different techniques used to create a more immersive world.


This shaped my desire to be involved with something that creates a meaningful experience, an experience shaped by the user and his needs. I always shared this passion for helping others, to explain in a way that that individual would understand. I find it extremely gratifying to see people grasp the concept of a product without much explanation.
