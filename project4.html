From a young age, I have been a Formula 1 enthusiast. The game enabled me to be immersed in a life that I could not live. One of the most important parts of the immersion is the Ai you are racing against.

As I lived in Namibia Online racing was rather difficult because of latency issues I faced. So, I took it upon myself to improve the Ai.

The game realised on 2nd October 2014 and I started tinkering around with the Ai mid-October. I was incredibly happy with my results. The final mod was released on the 10th of November 2014.

Once I started to race online I shared my file with fellow League racers and asked for opinions. Through multiple conversations and testing.

Final Mod

Through much talking with friends and fellow league drivers, I came on the conclusion that the aggression on the ai was too weak and we wanted to make the offline career mode racing more exciting so I changed the code on the research I had gotten from the previous discussion and through many trials, cars just went far too slow through corners and them just too uncommon for mechanical issues. I finally came to something that achieved what we looked for.

So, this has been increased and the chances of spinning out in the rain have been increased.

This mod significantly improves the aggression of ai drivers. They try to take the inside line more often or the outside line they do not back out of overtakes so easily and are determined to overtake. Reviews are mutely appreciated.

On the 10th of November 2014, I uploaded the mod and after 5 years it has accumulated over 1500 downloads.
